Which Witch?

Rhythm-Dating Sim

Godot Gdscript 2D In-development

Overview

Which Witch is a rhythm game with dating sim elements integrated in. You play as a young first year witch/warlock looking to break out of their shell, along the way, you'll meet tons of fun characters.

For this project, I was working with a team of four during the original game jam that this project came from. Which Witch came in first place during that game jam and that version is playable in brower with the links below. Currently, Which Witch has a team of three.

The game has a unique mix of rhythm game, where the player must hit notes that fly down a track to a beat, and dating sim, where the player must pay attention to the dialogue and conversate with their chosen witch/warlock. The overall game will involve a day/time system and players will be able to roam the witch academy to boost stats and prepare themselves for their next conversation.

As of October 2025, this project is in development and is expecting to fully release on steam and itch by 2027.
