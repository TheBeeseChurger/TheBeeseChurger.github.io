About Me

I'm a passionate young game developer/student with a focus on creating immersive and engaging gameplay experiences mixed with compelling narratives. My journey in game development started at a young age where I found a love for storytelling, and has since evolved into a comprehensive skill set spanning programming, creative writing, and design.

Currently pursuing my degree in Computer Science with a minor in English-Creative Writing at Southern Illinois University edwardsville. I've worked on multiple projects ranging from solo prototypes to team-based productions. I specialize in Unity with C# development and Godot with Gdscript development, with experience in gameplay systems, story design, and user experience.

When I'm not coding or writing up documents or stories, you can find me analyzing best sellers, participating in game jams, or exploring new and upcoming indie titles. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and collaborate with other creative minds to bring innovative game concepts to life.
